Bilal Sehgol



Hi! I'm a Brooklyn based Product and UI/UX Designer and a graduate from NYU Tisch School of Arts - Interactive Technologies Program (2021).

I've worked as a Product Manager for high growth startups based in Asia (TimeSvr.com) and Australia (1Form.com.au) and a Visual Designer for NYU.

My toolkit includes Sketch, Figma, InVision, HTML/CSS, Javascript and working knowledge of Arduino, Python, C++, SwiftUI and databases (Postgres, Influx).




TimeSvr is a cloud based virtual assistant, featured in NY Times Best Seller - The Personal MBA, Slate, Inc and GigaOm.
GrowthChart UI/UX for a mobile app replacing NYC Yellow Cards for babies born in the city (Sketch, Invision, Balsamiq)
uberCompost composting is no longer ugly and tedious (Design Research, Prototyping, Wireframes, Pitch Deck)
CovidChat Full-stack web app shares location & messages between quarantined individuals (Node.js, Sockets, Javascript, MongoDB)
PlantToDatabase Visualizing plant humidity, temperature and luminosity data via Graphana, Postgres, MySQL, Nodejs
