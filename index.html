Bilal Sehgol



Hi! I'm a Brooklyn based Creative Technologist.

I designed and developed for NYU while pursuing a Masters from NYU Tisch School of Arts - Interactive Technologies Program (2021).

I've worked for high growth startups based in Asia (TimeSvr.com) and Australia (1Form.com.au).

My toolkit includes Python, Sketch, Figma, InVision, HTML/CSS, Javascript and working knowledge of Arduino, NumPy, C++, SwiftUI and databases (Postgres, Influx).
